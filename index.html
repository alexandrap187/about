About this Project

This project was created for my thesis surrounding my love for music, and combining that with design. For this project, my class was presented with a list of objects. I chose a clock, and turned my music taste into a time-based playlist. All 24 hours have 6 songs that correspond with that specific time, based on what genres I felt went best for each time of day.
